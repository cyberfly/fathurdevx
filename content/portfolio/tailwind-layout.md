# Tailwind Layout

TailwindLayout is a collection of popular layouts and patterns made with Tailwind CSS.

At LaLoka Labs, I proposed the idea and built this site to serve as our Front End reference, and we decided to open source it.

### Stack

- NuxtJs 2 (VueJs)
- TailwindCSS
